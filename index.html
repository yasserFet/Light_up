Light up

We are SARL LightUp, an Algiers-based start-up project offering sustainable portable power solutions. Our mission is to empower power-dependent businesses, rural communities, and outdoor enthusiasts in Algeria with reliable and eco-friendly power sources. With a focus on safety and uninterrupted operations, we provide efficient alternatives to traditional generators.
